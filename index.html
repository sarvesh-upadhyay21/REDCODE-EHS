    Our Core Services

    Safety Audit & Compliance

    Conduct professional audits and inspections of buildings, fire systems, equipment, and records. Get certified reports and improvement plans.

    Life Safety / Fire Safety Management

    Design or upgrade your Life Safety / Fire Safety Management system. Update policies, conduct risk assessments, and improve preparedness and compliance.

    Security Services

    Comprehensive security planning, guard services, electronic surveillance and audit systems for premises, events and organizations.

    Traffic Management

    Design and execute safe and effective traffic flow and parking systems. Ideal for complexes, events, campuses, and commercial spaces.

    Disaster Management

    Plan, prepare and respond to disasters effectively. Includes drills, SOPs, resource management and emergency response training.

    Environment Health & Safety (EHS)

    Create and implement EHS policies. Monitor compliance and conduct training and audits to ensure workplace safety and sustainability.

    Facility Management

    Manage and maintain residential, commercial or industrial premises with janitorial, maintenance, vendor and utility support services.

    Surveillance Systems

    Install, maintain and monitor CCTV, biometric and advanced surveillance systems for 24/7 security coverage.

    Consultancy Services

    Expert advisory in risk mitigation, safety compliance, legal audits and regulatory approvals for public and private sector clients.

    Training & Certification

    Provide safety, fire, disaster, and EHS training modules with certification for teams, corporates, educational and public organizations.
