How We Work

REDCODE EHS:

Delivering Complete 360° Safety & Compliance Solutions

At REDCODE EHS, we provide a true 360° compliance solution by assigning a dedicated Life Safety Expert (MSEM, CSP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS, RCRA, DOT) to serve as your acting EHS Manager and Facilities Operations Consultant. We lead audit and survey readiness efforts, conduct gap analyses and benchmarking, and represent your organization during CMS, TJC, OSHA, EPA, and other regulatory audits—managing all due diligence, documentation, and post-survey compliance actions.

Our Core Expertise Includes:

  • ✔️ Acting as your Safety Manager or Director of EHS / Facilities Operations
  • ✔️ Audit Readiness, Gap Analysis & Mock Surveys
  • ✔️ EHS Program Development & Oversight
  • ✔️ Life Safety & Emergency Preparedness
  • ✔️ Fire Safety & Prevention
  • ✔️ Conduct inspections and testing to ensure compliance with Life Safety and Facilities Codes, including NFPA 101 & 99, as well as TJC, CMS, OSHA, and EPA regulations
  • ✔️ Construction Health & Safety
  • ✔️ Hazardous Materials & Waste Management
  • ✔️ Facilities Operations Management
  • ✔️ Environmental Site Assessments
  • ✔️ Regulatory Compliance (CMS, TJC, OSHA, EPA, NFPA, DOT & more)

With REDCODE EHS, you gain trusted compliance expertise—ensuring your organization remains safe, compliant, and always audit-ready.
