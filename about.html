About Us

REDCODE EHS – Your trusted partner in safety, compliance, and peace of mind.

We provide a true 360° compliance solution, assigning a dedicated Life Safety Expert (MSEM, CSP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS, RCRA, DOT) to serve as your acting EHS Manager and Facilities Operations Consultant. From audit preparation and mock surveys to inspections, testing, and plan of correction development, we make sure your organization is always compliant, audit-ready, and confidently represented during regulatory visits.

Our Story

With over 17 years of hands-on experience, our team of certified professionals holds industry-leading credentials including CSP, ASP, CHMM, CHFM, AEM, CEM, FPS, RCRA, and DOT, as well as advanced degrees in Emergency Management and Homeland Security, and Environmental Health.

Who We Are

Shahzad Khan MSEM, CSP, ASP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS

Founder & President – Senior EHS Consultant

Shahzad Khan is the Founder and President of REDCODE EHS LLC, bringing over 17 years of specialized experience in environmental health and safety (EHS), hazardous materials and waste management, and healthcare facilities compliance. He holds a master’s degree in emergency management and Homeland Security from Massachusetts Maritime Academy and has completed advanced EHS leadership training at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. A recognized leader in the industry, Shahzad holds multiple professional certifications, including Certified Safety Professional (CSP), Associate Safety Professional (ASP), Certified Hazardous Materials Manager (CHMM), Certified Healthcare Facility Manager (CHFM), and Fire Protection Specialist (FPS). His strategic vision and technical expertise drive REDCODE EHS’s mission to deliver innovative, compliant, and effective EHS solutions across complex operational environments. He is a recognized expert in healthcare and behavioral health compliance, construction safety, physical environment (EOC & Life Safety), HAZMAT operations, emergency management, and regulatory readiness. Shahzad has held critical safety roles with a Fire & Rescue Department, the Massachusetts Convention Center Authority, and the Massachusetts Department of Public Health’s Emergency Operations Center. His leadership at REDCODE EHS is driven by a commitment to practical, compliant, and industry-specific safety solutions.

Nasir A. Khan MBA

Director of Accounting and Finance

Nasir A. Khan brings over a decade of expertise in accounting and financial management. He holds an MBA in Accounting and Finance from the University of New Haven – West CT and has successfully led financial operations across diverse sectors. As Director of Accounting and Finance at REDCODE EHS, Nasir oversees all financial functions, including budgeting, reporting, and strategic planning, ensuring the company’s financial health and supporting its continued growth. His deep understanding of financial systems and operational efficiency plays a key role in advancing REDCODE EHS’s mission.
