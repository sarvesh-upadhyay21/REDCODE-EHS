Safety Audit Readiness & Mock Survey Support

REDCODE EHS:

Our certified Life Safety Professional (MSEM, CSP, ASP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS) offers comprehensive support to prepare your organization for upcoming safety audits and regulatory surveys.

Our Services Include:

Get Connect

Identify compliance gaps by thoroughly reviewing your existing safety plans, policies, and procedures.

Strengthen your documentation to align with TJC, CMS, EPA, NFPA, ICC, OSHA, FGI, ASHE, ASHRAE, ASME or other applicable standards.

Conduct realistic mock surveys to uncover potential issues before the official audit.

Educate your staff on compliance requirements and best practices.

Represent your organization during official audits, providing expert support and advocacy.

Address deficiencies and implement sustainable corrective actions.

Offer end-to-end management solutions by staffing your EHS, EOC, and Facilities Management teams—providing long-term peace of mind.

Ensure full audit readiness with a trusted partner who understands the complexities of healthcare safety and regulatory compliance.
