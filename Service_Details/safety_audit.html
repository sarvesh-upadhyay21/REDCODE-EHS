Life Safety Professional Services

Our certified Life Safety Professional (MSEM, CSP, ASP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS) offers comprehensive support to prepare your organization for upcoming safety audits and regulatory surveys.

Our services include:

  • Gap Analysis: Identify compliance gaps by thoroughly reviewing your existing safety plans, policies, and procedures.
  • Documentation Review & Improvement: Strengthen your documentation to align with TJC, CMS, EPA, NFPA, ICC, OSHA, FGI, ASHE, ASHRAE, ASME or other applicable standards.
  • Mock Surveys: Conduct realistic mock surveys to uncover potential issues before the official audit.
  • Training & Education: Educate your staff on compliance requirements and best practices.
  • Audit Representation: Represent your organization during official audits, providing expert support and advocacy.
  • Corrective Action & Follow-Up: Address deficiencies and implement sustainable corrective actions.
  • Ongoing EHS Management: Offer end-to-end management solutions by staffing your EHS, EOC, and Facilities Management teams—providing long-term peace of mind.

Ensure full audit readiness with a trusted partner who understands the complexities of healthcare safety and regulatory compliance.
