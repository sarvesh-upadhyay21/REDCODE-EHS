Fire Safety Compliance for Office Buildings

REDCODE EHS offers expert fire safety support to ensure your office building remains safe, compliant, and inspection-ready.

Our Services Include:

  • Fire Risk Assessments: Identify hazards, evaluate occupant risk, and recommend mitigation strategies.
  • Code Compliance Consulting: Ensure compliance with NFPA 101, IFC, and local codes; support AHJ coordination and alternate compliance methods.
  • Fire Protection System Review: Assess alarm, sprinkler, and suppression systems; verify NFPA 25/72 compliance; coordinate vendor ITM services.
  • Life Safety Documentation: Develop or update life safety plans, egress diagrams, and compliance records.
  • Drills & Evacuation Planning: Design fire drills and create clear, building-specific evacuation procedures.
  • Staff Fire Safety Training: Provide hands-on training in fire prevention, extinguisher use, and emergency response.
  • Inspection Readiness: Prepare for fire marshal/OSHA inspections and provide on-site regulatory support.
  • Hazardous Material Review (if applicable): Assess flammable or hazardous materials stored on-site.
  • Interim Life Safety Measures (ILSM): Develop ILSMs during renovations or if compliance gaps are identified.
