Emergency and Disaster Management

REDCODE EHS:

Expert Emergency & Disaster Management Services

At REDCODE EHS, our certified expert (MSEM, CSP, CHMM, CHFM, CEM) provides professional support to help your facility or business stay safe, secure, compliant, and inspection ready for The Joint Commission, CMS, and other regulatory agencies.

Our team of experienced Emergency Managers and specialists delivers customized solutions tailored to your organization’s specific emergency preparedness and disaster management needs.

Our services include, but are not limited to:

Get Connect
  • I. Design and Review of Emergency Management Committees, Policies, Plans, and Procedures: Tailored to your organization’s specific needs—ensuring compliance, coordination, and operational readiness.
  • II. Emergency Management Program (EM.09.01.01)
  • III. Emergency Management Leadership (EM.10.01.01)
  • IV. Hazard Vulnerability Analysis (EM.11.01.01)
  • V. Emergency Operations Plan
    • A. Planning (EM.12.01.01)
    • B. Six Critical Areas:
      • 1. Communications (EM.12.02.01)
      • 2. Staffing (EM.12.02.03)
      • 3. Patient Care and Clinical Support (EM.12.02.05)
      • 4. Safety and Security (EM.12.02.07)
      • 5. Resources and Assets (EM.12.02.09)
      • 6. Utilities (EM.12.02.11)
  • VI. Continuity of Operations Plan (COOP) (EM.13.01.01)
  • VII. Disaster Recovery Plan (EM.14.01.01)
  • VIII. Staff Education and Training Program (EM.15.01.01)
  • IX. Testing the Emergency Operations Plan (EM.16.01.01)
  • X. Evaluation of the Emergency Management Program (EM.17.01.01)

Partner with REDCODE EHS to strengthen your emergency preparedness, ensure compliance, and safeguard your people, assets, and operations.
