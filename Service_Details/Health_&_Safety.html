Health & Safety Programs

REDCODE EHS:

At REDCODE EHS, our certified and highly experienced Life Safety Professional (MSEM, CSP, ASP, CHMM, CHFM, FPS, RCRA, DOT) brings over 17 years of hands-on expertise and serves as your dedicated EHS Manager.

We help organizations design and implement health and safety programs that comply with CMS, TJC, EPA, NFPA, OSHA, ASHE, ASME, CDC, ASHRAE, and other applicable regulations—protecting employees, minimizing risk, and ensuring full regulatory compliance.

A written plan is just the beginning—effective implementation is what drives results. That’s why we tailor every program to fit your specific operations and provide the tools, training, and support needed to ensure it’s consistently applied in the field.
