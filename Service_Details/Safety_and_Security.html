Safety and Security

REDCODE EHS – Expert Public Safety Consulting Services
REDCODE EHS offers professional support to ensure your facility or business remains safe, secure, compliant, and inspection-ready. Our team of experienced Public Safety professionals delivers tailored solutions to meet your organization's unique safety and security needs.

Our Services Include:

Get Connect
  • Workplace Violence Committees: Establishment and/or review of committees, policies, plans, and procedures.
  • Committee Design & Review: Design and review of Workplace Violence Committee structure and operations.
  • Prevention Program: Workplace Violence Committee Prevention Program development and implementation.
  • Hazard Vulnerability Assessments (HVA): Identify and prioritize potential threats to safety and security.
  • Management Plan, Policies & Procedures: Development of a comprehensive Safety and Security Management Plan.
  • Routine Safety & Security Inspections: Conduct inspections using advanced survey techniques.
  • Facility Risk Assessments: Focus on identifying vulnerabilities in high-risk areas.
  • CCTV Monitoring & Optimization: Ensure surveillance systems are effective and fully functional.
  • Security Officer Training: Deliver training programs and conduct annual performance evaluations.
  • Patrolling Techniques: Develop and implement effective security patrol methods.
