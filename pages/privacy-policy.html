By using our Site, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

    Details how REDCODE EHS LLC collects, uses, and protects personal information from Users of our Site.

  2. 2. Legal Basis for Data Collection

    We process your personal information (“Personal Information”) based on the following grounds: • To fulfill contractual obligations • To deliver and improve our services • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements • To protect our legitimate business interests

  3. 3. Information We Collect

    We collect two types of data: a) Non-Personal Information Data that cannot be used to identify you, including: • Browser and device type • Operating system • Language preferences • Pages visited, clicks, and time spent on the Site b) Personal Information Information that can identify you, such as: • Name • Email address • Mailing address • Phone number • IP address • Device identifiers (e.g., MAC address, UUID) • Geolocation data

    We may collect Personal Information through: • Forms or registrations you complete on the Site • Interactions with our services • Third-party analytics providers or publicly available sources

Types of Cookies: • Session Cookies – Temporary, deleted when the browser closes • Persistent Cookies – Stored for future visits • Third-Party Cookies – Set by analytics or ad partners You can disable cookies via browser settings, but some features may not function properly.
