Mold: Prevention & Evaluation – Protecting Your Facility

  • REDCODE EHS Team
  • Aug 16, 2025
  • 0 Comments

At REDCODE EHS, we provide comprehensive mold prevention and evaluation services to protect your facilities, assets, and occupants. Our experts help reduce health risks, avoid costly repairs, and maintain compliance with environmental and safety standards.

Prevention

Rapid response is key after water intrusion. Using tools such as infrared imaging, moisture meters, and visual inspections, our specialists identify risks before they become major issues. This proactive approach helps schools, hospitals, and businesses stay safe and compliant.

Evaluation

We conduct detailed evaluations to guide facility managers and restoration teams with safe and effective remediation. Our assessments ensure informed decision-making, reducing long-term risks to both people and property.

Our Mold Services Include:

  • ✔ Visual mold assessments
  • ✔ Moisture mapping
  • ✔ Mold sampling (air, surface, bulk)
  • ✔ Site-specific Mold Remediation Plans
  • ✔ Post-remediation verification & clearance

“The best way to manage mold is prevention. At REDCODE EHS, we focus on stopping risks before they start.”

Whether for healthcare, education, or commercial settings, REDCODE EHS ensures mold prevention and evaluation are handled with expertise and compliance at the core.
